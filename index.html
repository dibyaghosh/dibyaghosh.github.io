Research in Scalable Community Detection

Over the past year, I've been working with Dr. James Brown of Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory on developing scalable randomized algorithms to extract community structure out of expansive genetic networks.My work is focused on determining higher-order interactions between variables through Random Forest ensembles and randomized partitioning algorithms
