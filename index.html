I am a first-year PhD student at UC Berkeley. My research focuses on learning to make decisions with reinforcement learning.

In 2019-2020, I spent a wonderful year as an AI resident at Google Brain, Montréal. Before that, I was an undergrad at UC Berkeley, where I was fortunate to co-create and teach Stat 140, and do research in deep RL and computational biology.

How to pronounce my name

My first name is pronounced Dibbo or Dih-bo. On legal documentation, my name is Dibya Jyoti Ghosh.
